About Us

Shiksha offers a variety of products and services, such as online courses, digital textbooks, educational games and simulations, learning management systems (LMS), virtual and augmented reality tools, and more. These technologies allow for personalized and adaptive learning experiences, as well as real-time feedback and analytics to track student progress.

Intermediate

Shiksha provides college readiness tools that help intermediate students prepare for college. These tools can include college counseling services, test preparation courses, and college application support.

Degree

Shiksha use artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning algorithms to personalize learning for each student. These algorithms analyze student performance and adapt the learning experience to meet each student's unique needs and learning style.

Post Graduation

Shiksha offers practice exercises and assessments to help intermediate students reinforce their understanding of concepts and identify areas where they need further review. These assessments can be used by teachers to track student progress and adjust their instruction accordingly.

Virtual and Augmented Reality Tools

Virtual and augmented reality tools provide an immersive learning experience that enables students to explore complex concepts and ideas in a more engaging way. These tools can be used to simulate real-life scenarios and create interactive learning environments.

Educational Games and Simulations

Shiksha creates games and simulations that provide an interactive and engaging learning experience. These games and simulations are designed to help students develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Learning Management Systems (LMS)

An LMS is a software platform that enables teachers to create, manage, and deliver educational content to students. LMSs allow teachers to create and deliver online courses, track student progress, and provide feedback.
